Quick Help

No bite windows showing?

Set your device time correctly. Bite windows are calculated offline from your local date/time and location.

Location missing on log entries?

Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Clinton Lake Fishing Almanac → set to While Using the App.

Then reopen the app and add a new entry.

Photos won't attach?

Allow Photos access: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Clinton Lake Fishing Almanac → Add Photos Only or Full Access.

FAQ

  • Does the app work offline?
    Yes. All features (bite windows, logbook, sparkline stats) work offline.
  • What data do you collect?
    None. We don't track you and we don't send your data anywhere.
  • Where is my data stored?
    On your device only (AsyncStorage). Photos stay in your Photos library; the app stores references.
  • How do I export my catches?
    Open Settings → Export to generate a file (CSV planned in a future update). Share or save it via the iOS share sheet.
  • How do I import my data?
    Open Settings → Import and choose a backup file you previously exported.
  • What permissions are required?
    Location (for GPS tagging entries) and Photos (for adding catch photos). The app works without them but some features will be limited.
  • How accurate are bite times?
    They're heuristic windows (sunrise/sunset + moon phase). Conditions on the water vary; always use local knowledge.
  • How do I report a bug or request a feature?
    Email drewcrichardson1999@gmail.com with steps to reproduce and screenshots if possible.

Troubleshooting

Reset permissions

Location: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Clinton Lake Fishing Almanac → While Using the App.

Photos: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Clinton Lake Fishing Almanac → select access level.

If data looks missing

  1. Make sure you didn't uninstall the app (that deletes local data).
  2. Check Settings → Import to restore from your backup.

Still stuck?

Email support with your iPhone model, iOS version, app version (see Settings → Diagnostics), and a short video or screenshots.

Open Settings → Diagnostics
